What Freightvera is being built to surface

As the platform grows, the goal is to surface the kinds of signals that actually matter when deciding whether to work with a company in freight.

Safety & compliance Relevant operating and compliance indicators that support better screening.
Business credibility Signals that help identify whether a company presents professionally and operates consistently.
Reputation patterns Helpful context around how a company is perceived by the market over time.
Risk indicators Early warning signs that may suggest elevated operational or fraud-related concern.
